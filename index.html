Core Technologies Explained

Secure & Tamper-Proof Identity

Our blockchain issues secure, temporary digital IDs with verified KYC. This prevents fraud and creates an immutable, trustworthy travel record for authorities.

Intelligent Monitoring & Alerts

AI models analyze travel patterns to detect anomalies like sudden location drops or prolonged inactivity. This proactive approach helps flag distress situations early for intervention.

Context-Aware Safety Zones

Geo-fencing creates virtual perimeters around high-risk zones. If a tourist enters a restricted area, an instant alert is sent to them and the authorities, providing a critical safety warning.

Feature Deep-Dive

The system provides tailored features for both tourists needing protection and the authorities providing it, creating a seamless and effective safety network.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Journey

This flow illustrates how the system operates from arrival to incident resolution, creating a proactive safety net that works seamlessly throughout a tourist's visit.

Step 1: On-Arrival Registration

Tourist's KYC is scanned at entry to generate a secure, temporary Blockchain Digital ID. They download the app.

↓

Step 2: Safe Exploration

The app runs in the background, updating the Safety Score, checking for geo-fence breaches, and monitoring for travel anomalies.

↓

Step 3: Incident Trigger

An issue is triggered by the Panic Button, AI-detected anomaly (e.g., inactivity), or a geo-fence breach.

↓

Step 4: Instant Alert & Dispatch

An immediate alert with the tourist's ID and location is sent to the central dashboard and dispatched to the nearest police unit.

↓

Step 5: Coordinated Response

Authorities use the dashboard to track the tourist's location and coordinate a response. Missing person cases get an auto-generated E-FIR.

↓

Step 6: Resolution & Departure

The incident is resolved. The Digital ID expires at the end of the trip, ensuring data privacy.
