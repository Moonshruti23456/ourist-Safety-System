Core Technologies Explained

Secure & Tamper-Proof Identity

We use a blockchain to issue secure digital IDs to tourists. These IDs contain basic KYC, itinerary, and emergency contacts, and are only valid for the trip's duration. This prevents identity fraud and ensures that travel records are immutable and trustworthy, providing a single source of truth for authorities.

Intelligent Monitoring & Predictive Alerts

Our AI models analyze travel patterns in real-time. The system detects anomalies like sudden location drop-offs, prolonged inactivity, or significant deviation from planned routes. This proactive approach helps flag potential distress situations before they escalate, allowing for early intervention.

Context-Aware Safety Boundaries

The system uses geo-fencing to create virtual perimeters around high-risk or restricted zones (e.g., sensitive border areas, dangerous trekking paths). If a tourist enters one of these zones, an instant alert is sent to both the tourist and the authorities, providing a crucial safety warning.

Ecosystem in Action: A Tourist's Journey

This flowchart illustrates how the system works from the moment a tourist arrives until an incident is resolved. Each step is connected, creating a proactive safety net that operates seamlessly in the background throughout the entire visit.

Step 1: On-Arrival Registration

Tourist arrives at an airport or hotel. Their KYC (Aadhaar/Passport) is scanned, and a secure, temporary Blockchain Digital ID is generated. They download the companion app.

‚Üì

Step 2: Safe Exploration

The tourist explores. The app runs in the background, updating their Safety Score and checking for entry into geo-fenced zones. The AI monitors for travel anomalies.

‚Üì

Step 3: Anomaly or Incident Occurs

A potential issue is triggered. This could be the tourist pressing the Panic Button, the AI detecting prolonged inactivity, or a breach of a geo-fenced high-risk zone.

‚Üì

Step 4: Instant Alert & Dispatch

An immediate alert with the tourist's Digital ID, location, and alert type is sent to the central dashboard and dispatched to the nearest police unit and the tourist's emergency contacts.

‚Üì

Step 5: Coordinated Response

Authorities use the dashboard to track the tourist's last known location and coordinate a response. If the tourist is reported missing, an E-FIR is automatically generated to begin a formal investigation.

‚Üì

Step 6: Resolution & Departure

The incident is resolved. At the end of the trip, the Digital ID automatically expires, ensuring all temporary data is secured and privacy is maintained.
